Our Commitment to You

At Pure Tech Painting, we are 100% committed to customer satisfaction. We take every measure to ensure that when we paint your home or office building that you are completely satisfied with the outcome. We understand that every job is different and will do everything necessary to make sure each painting project is done with the utmost care and precision.

From the Owner.....

At the young age of 14, I began my painting career working for my father who, at the time, owned his own painting company S&T Painting. During the summer months to make extra money, painting residential and commercial properties. I took the opportunity and worked with my father throughout my high school years learning a great deal from the experience. After high school i became a full time employee with S&T Painting. After S&T Painting closed it doors, I went to work for a few different painting company's and a few other other jobs in different industry.

Around the age of 38, I decided to obtained my contractor's license and made the decision to branch out on my own forming Pure Tech Painting. Now having been in the painting industry for over 20 years, Pure Tech Painting strives for quality workmanship and complete customer satisfaction. Being family owned and operated, I personally oversee each project ensuring that the work performed is of the highest quality. This is my promise to you..

We are proud to have built our business on customer referrals and repeat clients.

The biggest compliment we receive is repeat business and customer referrals. It means the work was done right, at a fair price and we have gained the trust and loyalty of our client.
