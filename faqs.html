Which paint brands does Pure Tech Painting use?

We have found that using economy grade paint labeled good, better, or contractor grade do not perform well. We use only premium paint products from trusted brands such as Kelly-Moore, Benjamin Moore, Sherwin-Williams, and Glidden. If you would prefer using a brand not listed, please let us know and we will accomodate your request.

Do you supply a detailed painting proposal?

Yes. We will provide a detailed proposal outlining all facets of the job.

Do you protect the homeowner’s property when painting?

Yes. Our crew will ensure that your home or office is protected from any excess paint, dirt or debris by covering all areas with plastic, drop cloths, masking paper and painter's tape. We encourage customers to view the completed interior and exterior house painting preparation work before any paint is applied.
