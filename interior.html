Getting Started With Interiors

No matter the size of your project, Pure Tech Painting can handle all of your interior house painting needs. Whether you have brand new construction that needs interior paint or you’re just seeking to brighten or freshen up your interior walls, hiring an interior painting service is a simple and effective way to get the painting job done. Of course, you could do it yourself but who wants to wrestle with drop cloths and stepladders if you can hire a professional to do it for you?

Pure Tech Painting can help you select the right colors and paint to completing the job in a timely fashion, you can count on us to treat your property with the care and the respect that it deserves. Paint can transform any room in your home. It's a great way to give your interior living space a quick yet impactful decorating makeover. Let Pure Tech Painting take the guesswork out of your painting project by scheduling a complimentary consultation today. 

Our Process

1. The Set Up
Here we take the steps necessary to protect your flooring, furniture and other items from being damaged. This includes covering the floor with drop cloths and moving all items to the center of the floor where they are covered by plastic sheeting.

2. Preparation
Next, our interior house painters prepare the walls and ceilings so the final results will look as good as new. This step may include sealing stains, scuff sanding the surface and filling holes and cracks.

3. Painting
Now that the room is properly prepared, it is time to prime the areas that have been repaired and to apply the paint.

4. The Clean Up
After the paint has been applied, we remove our materials and then move your furniture and accessories back to where you request. We then vacuum and sweep your floors to ensure the room is as clean, if not cleaner, than it was before we started.

5. The Inspection
Finally, we inspect our work and then ask you to perform an inspection to make certain our work has met your expectations.
